# Sales Video Research

Use this when the operator asks to analyze a VSL, extract a pitch, mine
objections, inspect a sales video transcript, compare pitch mechanisms, or
codify the sales argument into durable offer/conversion truth.

## Route

Keep the work in `/mb-think` when the task is Sense/Decide:

- teardown: identify hook, promise, mechanism, proof, objections, CTA, and gaps;
- objection mining: extract spoken objections and missing proof;
- mechanism work: separate the offer's actual mechanism from copy flourishes;
- codification: update decisions, research, offer, proof, or angle files after
  operator approval.

If the operator asks to write a page/lander/about video, hand off to `/mb-site`.
If they ask for paid video creative or variants, hand off to `/mb-ads`. If they
ask for clips or excerpts, hand off to `/mb-organic`.

## Shared References

Load `.claude/reference/conversion/vsl-routing.md` first. Load a long-form
framework only when structure helps the teardown:

- Skool / membership: `.claude/reference/conversion/vsl/skool-18-section.md`
- B2B high-ticket: `.claude/reference/conversion/vsl/b2b-haynes.md`
- B2B example: `.claude/reference/conversion/vsl/examples/b2b-ijanitorial.md`

## Output

Save research to `research/YYYY-MM-DD-sales-video-<slug>.md` or codify through
the normal decision/codify flow. Treat framework sections as analytical lenses,
not as a mandate to rewrite the script. Verify claims before they become offer,
proof, angle, or page truth.
